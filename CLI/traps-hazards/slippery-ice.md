# Slippery Ice
*Wilderness Hazard*  

Slippery ice is difficult terrain. When a creature moves onto slippery ice for the first time on a turn, it must succeed on a DC 10 Dexterity ([Acrobatics](/CLI/skills.md#Acrobatics)) check or fall [prone](/CLI/conditions.md#Prone).

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide p. 110*
